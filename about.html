ABOUT US

We, a group of students at STI College Ormoc, have developed an e-commerce app specifically tailored to our local community. Our app aims to provide fast and efficient delivery service within our city, making it easier for residents to shop for their everyday needs. We understand the challenges of finding reliable delivery options and we believe that our app addresses this issue by connecting customers with local merchants. We believe that by supporting local businesses and providing a convenient shopping experience, we can strengthen our community and foster economic growth. We are excited to launch our app and invite residents to try it out and see the difference for themselves.
